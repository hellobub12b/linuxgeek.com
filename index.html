Minecraft on linux for free!

What?!, Minecraft...on linux?!... for FREE?!: Yep, follow the steps below to get started!

Step 1: Installing Java 8

First, to open Minecraft for linux, java is mandatory for this.Open your terminal and type :

sudo apt install openjdk-8-jre

Then wait until it is done installing

Step 2: Installing Tlauncher

Next, you are going go to go this site

Tlauncher

Then click download and pick linux

Now open your files and look for the tlauncher file.Once you have found it, right click on it and select properties, click on the permissions section and chek mark the "execute file as a program" box

Then you are going to go back to the file, right click it, and select open with "Openjdk", then run it and minecraft will open

Bonus!:How to make a server!

To make a server, go to Aternos.

Aternos

Then hit the play button and sign up.Once you have signed up, click "create a server", then select the version (Bedrock or Java) that you minecraft is set to, (in this case, click java) then click create

To customize you server, click on the options section to the left, and customize it to however you like.

Next, click on start to launch your server.Once it says online in green, then click connect on the top and copy and paste the Dynamic Ip.if you want people to join you, share you ip with others.
